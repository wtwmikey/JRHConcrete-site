FAQs

What areas do you serve?

Our service area includes nearby communities in Boone County and Central Missouri. Contact us to confirm if we serve your location.

How long does a concrete driveway installation take?

expand_more

Most concrete driveway installations take 2-3 days from start to finish. This includes site preparation, form work, pouring, and finishing. Weather conditions may affect the timeline. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your free estimate.

Do you offer free estimates?

expand_more

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all concrete projects. Our team will visit your property, assess the project, and provide you with a detailed quote. There's never any pressure‚Äîjust honest, transparent pricing.

Are you licensed and insured?

expand_more

Yes, JRH Concrete & Construction LLC is fully licensed and insured. We carry both general liability insurance and workers' compensation to protect you and our team. We can provide proof of insurance upon request.

What types of concrete finishes do you offer?

expand_more

We offer a variety of concrete finishes including smooth, broom finish, stamped concrete, colored concrete, and decorative options. During your consultation, we'll help you choose the best finish for your project and budget.

How long does concrete need to cure?

expand_more

While concrete gains strength over time, it's typically safe for light foot traffic after 24-48 hours and vehicle traffic after 7 days. Full curing takes about 28 days. We'll provide specific recommendations based on your project and weather conditions.

Do you work with property managers and commercial clients?

expand_more

Yes, we regularly work with property managers, business owners, and commercial clients. We understand the unique needs of commercial projects including scheduling, coordination, and maintaining business operations during work.

When is the best time of year for concrete work?

expand_more

Concrete work can be done year-round, but spring through fall typically offers the best conditions. We work in various weather conditions and take appropriate measures to ensure quality results regardless of season.
